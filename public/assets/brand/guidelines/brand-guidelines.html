Brand Identity

MWDesign represents innovation, creativity, and professional excellence in digital design. Our brand reflects modern design principles while maintaining accessibility and user-centered focus.

Logo Usage

The MWDesign logo is the cornerstone of our visual identity. It should be used consistently across all touchpoints.

Logo Variations

  • Primary Logo: Full color horizontal version for most applications
  • Vertical Logo: For narrow spaces and mobile applications
  • White Logo: For dark backgrounds and overlays
  • Monochrome: Single color version for special applications

Typography

Primary Font: Inter - A modern, highly legible font perfect for digital interfaces.

Secondary Font: JetBrains Mono - Used for code blocks and technical content.

Logo Usage Guidelines

✓ DO

  • Maintain clear space around logo
  • Use approved color variations
  • Scale proportionally
  • Use high-resolution files

✗ DON'T

  • Stretch or distort the logo
  • Change logo colors
  • Add effects or shadows
  • Place on busy backgrounds
